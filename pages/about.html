Our Story

sikaMotion is a collective brand name for creative companies united by a common goal: money and success. We began on July 1st, 2025, inspired by our flagship idea SIKA LIFEGUIDE — a vision to create a lifelong map to success.

From this foundation grew SikaLifeguide@sikaMotion Group Of Companies (SL@SM GOC). What started as one idea now drives a growing group of creative businesses working to shape industries and empower individuals.
